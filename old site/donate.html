Spark Children Home is a non-profit organisation. Our goal is to give each of our special children every opportunity they deserve to reach their greatest potential in life.

We have been providing basic needs for spark children more than 5 years and we will continue to support these children to fulfill their dreams and careers.

We are solely dependent on private donations and occasional assistance from international organisations to continue our programs for the children.

Spark Children Home needs your kind assistance to continue our program for the children.

If you are interested to support our project with donation, please write to us at: spark.chhome@gmail.com or donate from the donate button below.

On behalf of our spark children and our entire members of spark children home, we extend sincere appreciation for your generosity.
