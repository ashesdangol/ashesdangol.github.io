SUPPPORT THE CHILDREN

Mission

  • To organize and develop various kind of awareness programs for people of backward society in remote areas.

  • To focus on professional training, skill development and technical training for the children of the organization to make them skilled to earn a living after they leave the children home.

  • To organize non formal education program for the children in addition to the education they get in their school.

  • To work in coordination with National Level organization and International level organization to develop and run spark children home programs and work towards promoting children rights and focus in education of children.

    • General Information

    Spark Children Home, established in the year 2008 is a Shelter Home consisting of seventeen children. We have been providing these children with shelter, education, and food. Most of the children in our institution are the victim families during the civil war in Nepal.
